DPI & Sensitivity

Find a stable DPI and in-game sensitivity that matches your hand movement. A typical osu! setup ranges from 400 to 1200 DPI with low sensitivity; preference and playstyle vary. Test on a large play area to avoid clipping.

Grip & Aim

Grip style (palm, claw, fingertip) affects control and micro-adjustments. Practice tracking and flicking drills to find the best combination for your grip.
