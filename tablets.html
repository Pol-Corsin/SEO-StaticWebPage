Tablet playstyles

Tablet players usually choose between drag and hover styles. Drag players keep contact with the tablet surface while moving, while hover players lift the pen between movements. Each style affects aim and muscle memory.

Area & Mapping

Mapping your tablet area to screen size changes sensitivity. Larger active area yields more precision; smaller area increases speed. Experiment with mapping and OS sensitivity to find the sweet spot.
